Things to Do in New York City

There are so many things to do in New York City. Here are some places that I think you should definitely go and check out.

Battery Park
Battery Park is located at the southern tip of Manhattan. There, you can enjoy beautiful views of the Hudson River, East River, Statue of Liberty, Governor's Island, and Lower Manhattan.

New York Transit Museum
The New York Transit Museum is located in Brooklyn, New York, dedicated to preserving and showcasing the history of public transportation in New York City. Housed in a decommissioned subway station, the museum features an extensive collection of vintage subway cars, buses, and related artifacts.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art
The Metropolitan Museum of Art, commonly known as "The Met," is located on Fifth Ave in New York City. It houses an extensive and diverse collection of art spanning over 5,000 years of human history.
